***Financial Aid 2020-2021 Year Information***

Each year before December 1st, it is HIGHLY recommended that everyone apply for FAFSA form 1098-T

For priority scholarship considerations, it is highly recommend that the form be completed BEFORE November 1st

If completed on time, most people should receive:
- Nevada Millennium Scholarship (if eligible)
- Pell Grant (Varies according to financial status)

Other CSN grant(s) include:
- Nevada Promise Scholarship
- CSN President's Scholarship

Other UNLV grant(s) include:
- Valedictorian Scholarship
- Provost's Scholarship
- Rebel Scholarship
- UNLV Tuition + Award

Financial Aid Programs:
- Work study
- Federal work study

FAFSA Website: https://studentaid.gov/
